Privacy Policy

Schedule My Tabs

Schedule My Tabs is a browser extension that allows users to schedule tabs or tab groups to open at custom times. The extension operates entirely within the user's browser and does not collect any personally identifiable information.

All user preferences, scheduled tasks, and settings are stored locally using Chromeâ€™s `storage` API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

Data Collection

Schedule My Tabs does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, web activity, or browsing history. It functions entirely on-device.
